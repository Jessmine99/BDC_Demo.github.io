# Lab 3
In this section, we create an Analytical Model that prepares the sales data for reporting and dashboards.

The model combines sales transactions with related information about customers and materials, creating a complete view of sales performance.

We also define additional business metrics, such as average order price, discounts, and revenue indicators. These help business users better understand how sales are performing.

Once the model is activated, it can be used directly in SAP Analytics Cloud to build interactive dashboards and analyze sales data from different perspectives.
